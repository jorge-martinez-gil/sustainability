# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and research quality.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and other inappropriate conduct are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report incidents by opening a private security advisory or contacting project maintainers through repository channels.
